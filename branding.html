Branding

How we're getting our brand to you

And why it's important


As a brand-new type of business, we will require vigorous branding to get our name out there in the first year to attract subscribers. World of BeerCraft will be advertised across various mediums to get our company name known to interested parties.


Initially, Facebook advertising is used. Using the marketing tool available, we found that we can target advertisements to Facebook users who are 20 years or older in the Vancouver area. The system estimates 5-28 people will like the advertisement per day, whereas the ad will run year-round. Total price for this venture is simply $7 per day, providing a cheap means of reaching a large volume of potential customers.


As our subscriber base starts to grow, we plan to begin door to door advertising to help get our name out there. This coupled with current subscribers spreading the company by word of mouth is expected to generate interest in the service.


Another method to promote our business is using our logo. Taking homage from the World of WarCraft logo which many people know, we can use this distinction to our advantage. As it shares a similar style, this logo is expected to stand out for most people, increasing interest. We plan to include our logo at partnered breweries so customers who normally buy their brews from the brewery directly will take note of our business and potentially subscribe. Additionally, our delivery vehicles will be outfitted with decals advertising World of BeerCraft to draw interest as they are driving around Vancouver making deliveries.


Understanding the risks of alcohol abuse, we at World of BeerCraft hope that our clients will always drink responsibly. As such, $3/mo from every subscription is donated to Alcoholics Anonymous to help those struggling with alcoholism. As a service that sells alcohol, we respect the decision of those who prefer sobriety, and want to show our support for those who make the choice to do so.
