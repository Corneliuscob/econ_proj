About Us

World of BeerCraft is a local beer subscription service that caters to the craft beer enthusiasts of Vancouver. Partnered with the various craft breweries in the Vancouver area, World of BeerCraft is built upon the basis of customer service at its finest. Started by three beer connoisseurs, clients will be matched with beer based on a survey and algorithm based on the founders’ knowledge.

World of BeerCraft is not a physical store that one would pick up their craft beers from. Rather, the company is web based where users sign up for delivery. Subscribers receive a 6 pack of various craft beers each month. Each beer is chosen based on the algorithm and is unique to that person’s answers. At the start of the next cycle, the delivery personnel will pick up the used cans and drop off another 6 cans. These may be the same six flavours or may vary depending on the follow-up survey which may update your preferences.

All the craft beers are locally sourced from breweries in the Vancouver region so, subscribers are helping these breweries through this service. Not only this but getting the opportunity to sample various styles and flavours will allow you to learn more about your preferred tastes and what sort of beers you enjoy the most.
