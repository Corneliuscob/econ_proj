About Us

Colton Smithson, Ngai To Lo, Alvin Cheung


World of BeerCraft is a local beer subscription service that caters to the craft beer enthusiasts of Vancouver. Partnered with the various craft breweries in the Vancouver area, World of BeerCraft is built upon the basis of customer service at its finest. Started by three beer connoisseurs, subscribers will be delivered beer based on their preferences through a survey and data analytics after subsequent feedback of that month's beer.


World of BeerCraft is not a physical store that one would pick up their craft beers from. Rather, the company is web based where users sign up for delivery. Subscribers receive a 6-pack of various craft beers each month. Each beer is chosen based on the algorithm and is unique to that person’s answers. At the start of the next cycle, the delivery personnel will pick up the used cans and drop off another 6-pack. These may be the same 6 flavours or may vary depending on the follow-up survey which may update your preferences.


From Townsite Brewing in Powell River, to Fuggles and Warlocks in Richmond, subscribers are able to explore all the different craft beer that the Greater Vancouver region has to offer. This intimate adventure will allow subscribers to discover their palates and tastes through the different medium of breweries around the Vancouver region. Subscribers will see the aspects that make a lager, a lager and be able to experience the nuances and uniqueness of flavour that each brewery imparts into each bottle. All the craft beers are locally sourced from breweries in the Greater Vancouver region so, subscribers are helping these breweries through this service.
