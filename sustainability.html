Sustainability

Steps towards a greener future

And how we're doing our part!


As one of the most sustainable cities in North America, sustainability is a large factor for companies based in Vancouver. At World of BeerCraft we strive for sustainable practices wherever possible.


As a web-based company, all our receipts and paperwork are handled online, by default, to reduce paper usage; although, subscribers can opt for paper forms if they so desire. Packaging for beers is done using cardboard instead of 6-pack yokes which devastate aquatic life when disposed of improperly. We discourage the use of plastic bags and opt for carboard boxes when picking up from breweries rather than plastic.


GHG (Green House Gas) emissions are a major contributor to global warming and our transportation network adds to this issue. To help reduce emissions, all our delivery vehicles will be hybrids, namely the Toyota Prius C. As a hybrid vehicle, less gas is needed to run which means less GHGs are produced. While we are still contributing to global warming, the rate at which we do so is greatly reduced than if we used older model cars that solely rely on gasoline. Electric vehicles were considered to eliminate the reliance on gasoline. This was not feasible due to them being outside of our price range, lack of charging infrastructure around the property where we store our beer and the high depreciation rate of electric vehicles.


One of the features of our service is the pickup and proper disposal of the previous month’s cans. By doing so, we at World of BeerCraft ensure that the aluminium cans are properly recycled and do not end up in the landfill. To incentivise this initiative, we offer subscribers a $2 discount for people who return their empty cans during the next delivery. It is speculated most if not all people will take us up on this incentive as returning 6 beer cans only yields $0.60, so it is more economical if they return it to us. From our own economic standpoint, although we are making a loss due to this incentive, it does not put us below our MARR and improves company image.
